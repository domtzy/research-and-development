// 03 — Launch Process

How to Launch Step-by-Step

Anyone can do this in 30 minutes on pump.fun. Here are the 5 steps to go from zero to a live meme coin on Solana.

1

Create a Crypto Wallet

Download Phantom (for Solana) from phantom.app. It's free. Save your seed phrase somewhere safe — never share it. Fund it with $50–200 of SOL(Optional for Dev Buy).

Use Phantom wallet for the best pump.fun integration on Solana.
2

Go to pump.fun

The easiest meme coin launcher on Solana. No coding needed. Cost: $0. Connect your wallet and click "Create a coin."

pump.fun handles all the smart contract deployment automatically.
3

Fill in Your Coin Details

Name, ticker symbol (e.g. $DOGE), description, upload your logo/image. Make it catchy! This is what people will see. Add social links, website, and Telegram.

Coin data (social links, banner, etc) can only be added at creation — can't be changed after.
4

Launch & Buy First Tokens

After paying the fee, your coin is live! Immediately buy some yourself — this shows early demand and starts price activity. This is called a "dev buy" and is optional but recommended.

Buying small early helps protect your coin from snipers at launch.
5

Share Everywhere Immediately

Post your contract address on X (Twitter), Telegram, Reddit. Speed matters — post within minutes of launch. Use trending hashtags like #Solana #memecoin.

The first hour after launch is critical. Momentum is everything in meme coins.

What is Solscan?

Solscan is a blockchain explorer (like a search engine) specifically for the Solana network. It acts as a public ledger where anyone can look up "digital receipts" for crypto transactions.

Verify Transactions — Check if a send/receive was successful using a Signature (TXID)
Track Wallets — Paste any address to see SOL balance, token list, and history
Token Audit — Search a coin's Mint Address to see total supply and market cap
Monitor Holders — View Holders tab to see if whales own most of a coin

// 07 — Selling Strategy

How to Sell Successfully

Making money is about WHEN you sell, not just how high the price goes. You play two roles depending on whether you're the creator or an investor.

👨‍💻 As the Creator

  • Buy tokens early at a low price
  • Hold while community grows
  • Sell in small portions as price rises — NEVER dump all at once
  • Keep 10–20% for long-term hype

📈 For Investors Buying In

  • Buy small at start, test the water
  • Set a target (e.g. 5x, 10x)
  • Use stop-loss — meme coins crash fast
  • Take profits in stages (sell 25% at 3x, 50% at 5x)
⚠️ WARNING — Rug Pulls Never "rug pull" (drain liquidity and run). It's unethical, destroys trust, and may be illegal in your country. Build real hype and a community that grows sustainably.

// 10 — Risk Management

Risks & Legal Considerations

Know this BEFORE you launch — protect yourself and understand the landscape.

99% of Meme Coins Fail

Most coins go to $0 within weeks. You need real community and timing. Never invest money you can't afford to lose.

Fraud & Rug Pulls are Illegal

If you promise returns or manipulate price to scam investors, this is securities fraud in many countries. Serious legal risk.

Market is Unpredictable

Even coins with great communities can crash overnight. Crypto markets move fast — be ready for extreme volatility.

Smart Contract Bugs

If you add custom code (not needed on pump.fun), bugs can be exploited. Use audited templates only.

Tax Obligations

Crypto gains are taxable in most countries. Keep records of all buys and sells. Consult a crypto-friendly accountant.

Community Trust

Your reputation is everything in crypto. Be transparent, deliver on what you say, and never abandon your community.
